How to Play

Tap SPACE, ↑, or X to make the ball jump. Guide it through the center of the hoops to score points. Miss a hoop or hit the ring, and it's game over!

Power-ups

🛡️ Shield: Protects you from one collision
⚡ Speed Boost: Temporarily slows down hoops
⭐ Star: Double points for 5 seconds

Game Modes

Easy: Wider hoops, slower speed, frequent power-ups
Normal: Standard challenge
Hard: Faster hoops, tighter spacing, rare power-ups
