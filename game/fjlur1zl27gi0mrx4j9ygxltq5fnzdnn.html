REALITY OF COSMOS

Sector Run: Survive, Collect, and Face the Abyss Gate.

HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

Mobile: Use the on-screen buttons.
• ▲ / ▼ = move up / down
• LASER = shoot
• ❚❚ = pause

PC:
• W / S or ↑ / ↓ = move up / down
• SPACE = shoot
• ESC = pause

MISSION GOALS

• Destroy asteroids to gain score.
• Reach 50 score to warp to the next sector.
• Reach 120 score to unlock the Boss at the Abyss Gate.

SURVIVAL

• If an asteroid hits you, you lose a heart.
• Orange spheres restore health (up to 5 hearts).

TIPS

• Keep moving to dodge incoming asteroids.
• Focus fire on the Guardian during the boss battle.
