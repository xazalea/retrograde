How to Play

Objective & Strategy

  • Jump between platforms to survive and collect items for points.
  • Every jump costs energy—longer jumps cost more. Plan the shortest path to conserve your power!
  • You must collect items to recharge. Prioritize them in this order for maximum energy: Stars > Energy Pills > Energy Rings.

Watch Out for Traps!

  • Unstable Platforms: Some platforms will collapse after you land on them. Be ready to jump away quickly, as they disappear much faster in higher levels!

Mobile

Tap on a platform to jump to it.

PC Controls

  • Click a platform to jump.
  • Use A/D or Arrow Keys to slide on a platform.
  • Press Space Bar for a smart-jump to the nearest platform (prefers rewards).
  • Press R to restart after Game Over.
