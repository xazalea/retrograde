3D Racing Typing Game

How to Play

Type the words that appear on screen as fast as you can.

Each correct word gives you a speed boost and fills your nitro bar.

You have 10 seconds to type each word. If time runs out, it counts as a mistake!

Complete 250 levels to finish the race. Good luck!
