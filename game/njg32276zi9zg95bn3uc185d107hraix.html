About Dots and Boxes 3.0

Dots and Boxes is a classic strategy game where players take turns connecting dots to form boxes. The player who completes the most boxes wins!

How to Play:

  • Click on a line between two dots to claim it
  • Complete a box by drawing its fourth side
  • When you complete a box, you get another turn
  • The game ends when all boxes are completed
  • The player with the most boxes wins!

Features:

  • Play against AI (Normal, Hard, or Expert difficulty)
  • Two-player local multiplayer
  • Customizable board sizes (up to 20x20)
  • Quick Game 40% board fill option
  • Game statistics tracking
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • GameMonetize SDK integration

What's New in v3.0:
