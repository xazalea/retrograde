How to Play

Objective: Grow your snake by eating the colorful food pellets.

Controls:

  • On Desktop: Move your mouse to steer your snake. Hold spacebar to boost.
  • On Mobile: Use the virtual joystick to move and the boost button to speed up.

Be careful! If your head touches another snake's body, you die.
Collect special power-ups for an advantage!
