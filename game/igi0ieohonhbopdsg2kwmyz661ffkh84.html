💡 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Match all pairs of cards in the fewest moves and shortest time possible.

📜 Game Rules

  • Click a card to reveal the emoji behind it
  • Click a second card to find its matching pair
  • If the cards match, they stay revealed
  • If they don't match, both cards flip back
  • Remember positions to find matches faster!

🏆 Scoring System

Your score is calculated based on moves and time. Fewer moves and less time = higher score!

⚙️ Game Options

  • Board Size: 4×4 (Easy), 6×6 (Medium), 8×8 (Hard)
  • Difficulty: Controls how long mismatched cards stay visible
