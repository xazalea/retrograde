How to Play

Click/tap to cast a net. Catch fish for score, coins and energy.

Avoid dangerous fish (Lionfish, Shark, Octopus). Watch out for events like the Energy Rush!

⭐ COMBO SYSTEM (Unlocks at Level 3) ⭐

Catch good fish quickly to build a combo and multiply your score!
(3+ fish = x2 score, 6+ fish = x3 score, etc.)
Hitting a danger fish or waiting too long will break your combo.
