How to Play

Break the bricks with the ball like classic Arcanoid. Use your side guns to shoot enemy ships that descend straight down. The ball also damages enemies and the boss.

  • Move: Mouse (move), or ◀ ▶
  • Shoot guns: Mouse Click or Space (fires from left & right cannons)
  • Pause: P   Mute: M
  • Goal: Clear bricks, survive waves. Every 12 kills spawn a boss. Boss shoots one aimed shot.
  • Lives: 10 balls. Getting hit or letting a ship pass costs a life.
