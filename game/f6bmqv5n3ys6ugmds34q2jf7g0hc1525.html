About AquaSort

AquaSort 2 is a relaxing color sorting puzzle game. The goal is simple: sort the colored liquids into bottles so that each bottle contains only one color.

How to Play

Tap a bottle to select the top layer of color, then tap another bottle to pour the color into it. You can only pour into an empty bottle or on top of the same color, and only if there's enough space.

Developed by Ihor Khodakivskyi with love & a splash of color! We hope you enjoy the game.
