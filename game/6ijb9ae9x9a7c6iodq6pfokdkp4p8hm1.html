HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Stack falling pieces to complete horizontal lines

Completed lines disappear and give you points

Game ends when pieces reach the top


💎 SCORING SYSTEM

1 Line = 40 × Level

2 Lines = 100 × Level

3 Lines = 300 × Level

4 Lines (TETRIS) = 1200 × Level


🔥 BONUS SYSTEM

COMBO: Clear lines consecutively without placing pieces that don't clear lines. Each combo level gives +50% bonus!

BACK-TO-BACK TETRIS: Clear 4 lines twice in a row for an extra +50% bonus!


📈 LEVELING UP

Clear 10 lines to advance to the next level

Higher levels = faster falling pieces

Higher levels = more points per line


💡 TIPS

• Use ghost piece (gray outline) to see where pieces will land

• Save the I-piece for TETRIS (4 lines at once)

• Keep your stack low and flat

• Plan ahead using the NEXT piece preview
