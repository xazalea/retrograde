Mission Briefing

Objective

Survive against waves of enemies and compete for the highest kill count. In Multiplayer, be the last one standing or reach the 10-death limit first!

Desktop Controls

  • W A S D Move
  • SPACE Jump
  • MOUSE Look & Aim
  • L-CLICK Shoot
  • ESC Unlock Cursor / Pause

Mobile Controls

Use the on-screen joysticks:

  • Left Side: Drag to Move
  • Right Side: Drag to Look
  • Tap Buttons: Shoot & Jump

Pro Tips

• Aim for the HEAD for 2x damage!

• Collect green potions to heal.

• Warning: Big Boss appears at 15 kills.
