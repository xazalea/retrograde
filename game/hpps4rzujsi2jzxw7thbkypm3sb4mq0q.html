ℹ️ About Cosmic Defender

🎮 Game Description

Cosmic Defender 2.2 is the Enhanced Protocol space shooter where you defend against relentless alien invaders. Battle through multiple levels, defeat powerful bosses, and prove your skills as the ultimate cosmic defender!

📖 How to Play (Enhanced)

  • Move: Use your mouse cursor, A / D keys, or ← / → arrow keys. **On Mobile**, drag your finger horizontally.
  • Fire: Hold left mouse button, press and hold SPACE, or **tap/hold on mobile** to engage continuous fire.
  • Pause: Press the **☰ Menu** button, the P key, or the Esc key.
  • Objective: Clear all enemy waves and defeat the Boss at the end of each level.
  • Lives: You start with 3 lives. Gain +1 life for starting a new level (max 6).
  • Leveling: Progress through 6 core levels, then face the **FINAL BOSS**.

👾 Enemy Types (V2.2 Specs)

  • Small (Interceptor): 1 HP • Fast • 30 points
  • Medium (Cruiser): 2 HP • Balanced • 20 points
  • Heavy (Juggernaut): 3 HP • Tough • 12 points
  • Elite (Phantom): 4 HP • Advanced • 40 points

🎯 Boss Mechanics

  • **NEW:** Power-up system (Rapid Fire, Shield)
  • Bosses are **Invulnerable** until all standard enemies are destroyed.
  • Bosses have regenerating shields and increasing HP each level.
  • They use multiple attack patterns (Salvo & Spiral).
  • Bosses **Enrage** when alone, becoming faster and deadlier.
  • Final boss has 6x HP and special radial burst attacks.

✨ Features

  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Dynamic boss battle patterns
  • Enhanced Visual and Sound effects
  • Statistics tracking
  • Customizable settings
  • **FIXED:** Robust Pause/Resume logic

🏆 Tips

  • Prioritize Elite enemies for maximum points.
  • Don't let enemies reach the bottom of the screen (you lose a life!).
  • Stay mobile to avoid dense boss bullet patterns.
